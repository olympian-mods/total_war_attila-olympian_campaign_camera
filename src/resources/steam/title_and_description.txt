TITLE
-----

Olympian Campaign Camera


DESCRIPTION
-----------

[h1]Mod description[/h1]

A campaign camera mod for Total War: Attila with adjusted height parameters.
It allows the camera to zoom further out on the campaign map and a bit closer down to ground level.
